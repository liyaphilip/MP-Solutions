History
MP Solutions was started by few B.Tech students in the year 2010. There first office was at Kochi, Kerala.Then they shifted the headquarters to Bangalore,Karnataka. It was a Startup which was later developed as one of the largest IT company. Its initial capital was $300. It was registered as MP Solutions in 25 April 2010.

It made a public issue of the shares in the year 2016.Each share costed an amount of $10. The annual revenue of the company in 2018 was $1500. It becomes as one of the most fasting growing company in India.

It had around 200 employees in the year 2015.Later the number of employees increased and reached to 800 in 2018. It is one of the largest IT company in India. It had around 150 offices around the world. It had collabrations with many foreign companies.
